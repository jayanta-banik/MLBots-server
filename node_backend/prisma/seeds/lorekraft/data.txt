Attributes:
Strength: 8
Agility: 7
Defense: 8 (Physical Defense: 9, Magical Defense: 7)
Magic: 7
Intelligence: 7
Weapon of Choice: Petrifying Gaze – The Gorgon’s gaze can turn any creature to stone upon direct eye contact.
Abilities:
Petrify (Regular): The Gorgon attempts to turn an enemy to stone with her gaze. On a failed saving throw, the target begins to turn to stone and becomes immobilized.
Snakes’ Bite (Heavy, 2-turn reload): The Gorgon’s snakes lash out, biting all nearby enemies and poisoning them dealing damage over time.
Stone Form (Unique Circumstance - When in danger): The Gorgon can temporarily turn herself to stone, becoming immune to all damage for one turn.
Passive Ability: Petrifying Aura – Enemies within 10 feet of the Gorgon must make a saving throw for each turn or begin to feel sluggish. If failed to make a saving throw for the next turn will be stuned, loosing that turn
